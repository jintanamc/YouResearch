Fellowship


The 2018 Fellowship application will open in January. In the meantime, click here to learn about the 2017 Class of Fellows!

The YouResearch Fellowship is a match-and-mentorship program that connects selected high school juniors and seniors with research professors in their area of interest. In addition, each Fellow is assigned one of the YouResearch team's experienced student advisors who will work directly with the student throughout the entire research process.

Eligibility Requirements

Preference is given to high school juniors and seniors. However, applications from all grade levels will be considered.

Application Process

Each year, the YouResearch committee carefully reviews applications and determines the high school students who are prepared for advanced research based on their academic performance, extracurricular involvement, volunteer work, and more. After the holistic review, the committee will reach out to research facilities in their area to find a laboratory suited to their interests.

1. Complete the online application

The 2018 YouResearch Fellowship application will open in January 2018. By submitting this application, you provide permission for the YouResearch committee to review your transcripts and acknowledge that you meet the eligibility requirements.

2. YouResearch Fellow Selection

As mentioned, the YouResearch committee will undergo a detailed review of your application and release decisions in April.

3. Scholar Placement

If placed, you begin research in your area in June. You work directly with a university professor, PhD student, or a industry worker on research and have a designated YouResearch Associate who will provide assistance until the end of the program in July.
